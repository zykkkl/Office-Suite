# General Purpose

> Domain: Meeting presentations, project introductions, team updates, internal communications
> Cognitive posture: Balanced processing -- neither high-effort analytical nor low-effort emotional

## Density Target: Balanced (55-70%)

Each slide presents one complete topic with sufficient evidence to be self-contained. The audience should extract the key message within 5 seconds and understand the supporting evidence within 15 seconds.

## Visual-Verbal Ratio

Approximately 50% semantic text, 35% visual evidence (charts, structured graphics), 15% spatial breathing room. Every text-only slide is a missed opportunity for dual-channel encoding.

## Color Derivation

Temperature: neutral-refined. The palette should project competence without emotional bias.

| Token | Default | Rationale |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| accent | User's brand color, or a warm amber `#C9A84C` | Creates sufficient salience without aggressive warmth |
| bg_primary | Near-black `#0A0E1A` or clean white `#FFFFFF` | Context-dependent; dark for premium, white for clarity |

Maximum 4 distinct color values across the entire deck (including grayscale).

## Typography Posture

Titles carry conclusion statements, not topic labels. The H1 text on each slide should be readable as a standalone claim ("Q3 Revenue Up 23%") not a chapter heading ("Financial Results").

Body text at 13pt, never below 12pt. The audience reads body text only after the title and visuals have established context.

## Narrative Convention

Each slide's title is its conclusion. The body provides evidence. This inverts the traditional "topic -> detail" pattern into "conclusion -> justification", reducing the audience's time-to-insight.

## Prohibited Patterns

| Pattern | Why It Fails |
|---------|-------------|
| Text-only slides | Eliminates the visual processing channel |
| More than 4 colors | Fragments semantic color coding |
| Decorative icons without information content | Consumes spatial budget without adding meaning |
| Shadow and gradient effects | Increases visual noise without improving hierarchy |
| Font sizes below 12pt | Unreadable in projection environments |
